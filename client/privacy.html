Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

HIPAA Compliance & The Medical TL;DR

MedTranslate does not permanently store, log, sell, or transmit your audio or translation history to third parties for marketing purposes.

As a tool designed by clinicians for clinicians, patient privacy is paramount. Your microphone audio is processed strictly in real-time for the sole purpose of translating speech. Once a session ends, the audio data is discarded.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the translation service:

  • Microphone Audio: When you tap the microphone button or hold the push-to-talk interface, we collect audio to transcribe and translate your speech. This access is strictly requested at the moment of use and granted via your device's native permission system.
  • Usage Data: We may temporarily handle the text transcriptions (the source language text and the translated text) required to render them on your screen and generate the Text-to-Speech audio response.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for functional purposes:

  • To transcribe your spoken language in real-time.
  • To interface with translation algorithms to provide accurate medical translations.
  • To generate synthesized audio responses for the patient.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not build profiles on you or your patients.

  • Audio data is processed ephemerally. It is not recorded to permanent storage databases.
  • The translation "History Log" you see in the app is stored locally on your specific device's browser memory (or app state) during an active session so you can review and replay recent statements. Closing or refreshing the application wipes this temporary history.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide high-fidelity translations and speech synthesis, MedTranslate routes text and audio data through necessary infrastructure providers (such as WebSockets and native Speech APIs). These services process data as a conduit and are bound by their respective privacy agreements not to misuse ephemeral translation data.

5. Your Rights & Device Permissions

You retain full control over the app's access to your device:

  • You may revoke Microphone access at any time through your iOS Settings or Android Settings panels. (Note: Doing so will disable speech-to-text functionality).

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to clarify our practices or reflect new app capabilities. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the data practices of MedTranslate, please contact us at: support@theaidoc.ai.
